PROPHET EBENEZER A. FASHADE

FOUNDER & PRESIDENT, GOD OF SOLUTION

INT'L MINISTRY


The C&S God of Solution international Ministry is a Bible believing church
under the leadership of MST. Sup. Apostle Prophet Ebenezer Fashade.

The church was established in 2017 and was rededicated in 2020,
We are a spiritual family church rooted in Gods word and love for
the community. We are dedicated to spreading Gods love to his people.
We take the well being of the community at heart which has inspired
us to continue to help the less privileged, the orphans and the widows.

Our goal to make education and healthcare easily accessible to the community
by building schools and hospitals to care for people who cannot afford medical
expenses. We are known for our yearly program tagged “The blood of Jesus”
a seven day program that holds at the end of every year where healing and great
testimonies are recorded. Love is our foundation and we want to continue to spread
God’s love all over the world.

WHO IS EBENEZER FASHADE ?

MST. Sup. Apostle Prophet Ebenezer Ayodeji Fashade was born in Oshogbo, Nigeria into a C&S home. Prophet Ebenezer is from a family blessed with so many
spiritual gifts.
He is blessed with the gift of song, interpretation of tongues, vision, dreams; speaking and singing in tongues. He was raised in a family rooted in God's word and teaching.
Two of his siblings are also prophets. He was ordained and anointed as a prophet many years ago under the leadership of Baba Aladura Iwayeye. He was later ordained again
under the leadership of his spiritual father Baba Aladura Agba Emmanuel Aniyikaye Adedamola Adetoro JP of The Crusader Church international C&S Church Ipaja, Majiyagbe,
Lagos Nigeria where he worshipped for many years before relocating to the United States of America.

Prophet Ebenezer lived his life from childhood serving and doing the work of God. He was a member of the Salvation Army and the children’s choir in Nigeria.
He received inspiration from God Almighty several years ago to build a sanctuary where God's people can be delivered through the power of prayer and to spread the gospel
all over the world. He continues to spread the gospel and impact lives through his ministrations at different mountains. Prophet Ebenezer moved to the United States
several years ago where he continues doing the work of God and impacting lives through
the power of the holy spirit. He has been to several different states in the U.S. and countries where he participated
in revivals and preached the Gospel.

Prophet Fashade's goal is to ensure that God’s love is impacted through him to God's people all over the world.
